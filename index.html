About Us

Welcome to Nodavis Inc.—your trusted gateway to expert Japan travel consulting, private tour guidance, and real-estate investment services.

I’m Takuma Okamoto, founder and CEO. Born in Japan, I spent more than a decade in California and Washington, earning my U.S. CPA license and blending Western transparency with the spirit of Japanese hospitality. After returning to Tokyo, I added licensed real-estate expertise and Tokyo relocation services to my skill set, guiding overseas buyers through every step of the purchase process.

Today, Nodavis unites these strengths—finance, property, and deep cultural insight—to craft private tours and consulting services for international travelers and investors. From curated ramen safaris through Tokyo’s back-street izakaya alleys to hands-on property viewings in Tokyo and beyond, every itinerary is built on three promises:

  • Transparency – Clear pricing, straightforward advice, no surprises.
  • Authenticity – Locally vetted experiences you won’t find on mass-market platforms.
  • Reliability – Guidance backed by CPA-level diligence and licensed real-estate practice.

Whether you’re seeking a once-in-a-lifetime adventure or a savvy property investment, our bilingual team makes sure you feel informed, safe, and inspired—every step of the way.
